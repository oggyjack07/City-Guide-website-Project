Tekri Dewas - Maa Chamunda Mandir

Dewas ki Tekri, Madhya Pradesh ke Dewas sheher mein sthit ek pavitra sthal hai jo Maa Chamunda aur Tulja Bhawani ko samarpit hai. Yah mandir pahadi par bana hai, jahan tak pahuchne ke liye log paidal chadhai karte hain ya ropeway ka use karte hain. Mandir se sheher ka sundar drishya dikhai deta hai. Navratri ke samay yah sthal bhakton se bhar jaata hai aur ek shaktishaali dharmik aur adhyatmik anubhav prastut karta hai.
