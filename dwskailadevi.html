Kaila Devi Mandir - Shakti aur Bhakti ka Prateek

Kaila Devi Mandir Dewas ka ek pavitra aur prasiddh mandir hai jo maa Kaila Devi ko samarpit hai. Yah mandir un bhakton ke liye vishesh mahatva rakhta hai jo maa ke charanon me shraddha ke saath aate hain. Mandir ka vaataavaran shaant aur divya hai, jahan aarti ke samay bhakton ka sangh har dil ko bhav-vibhorr kar deta hai. Navratri ke samay yahaan bhari sankhya me log darshan ke liye aate hain. Mandir ke paas prasad ke thele aur chhoti dukaanen bhi milti hain.
