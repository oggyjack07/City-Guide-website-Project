Shri Mahakaleshwar Jyotirling, Ujjain

Shri Mahakaleshwar Mandir Ujjain ka ek pavitra aur prachin mandir hai, jo 12 Jyotirlingon me se ek hai. Yah mandir Shiv Bhakton ke liye ek ati vishesh sthal hai jahan har din hazaron bhakt darshan ke liye aate hain. Mahakal ki Bhasma Aarti subah-subah hone wali ek vishesh aarti hai, jise dekhna ek adhyatmik anubhav hota hai. Mandir ki bhavyata, shanti aur bhakti se bhara hua vatavaran man aur atma dono ko shuddh kar deta hai.
