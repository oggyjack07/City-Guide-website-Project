Rajwada - Indore Ka Itihaas

Rajwada Indore ke beechon-beech sthit ek prachin aur bhavy mahal hai, jise Holkar rajvansh ke dwara 18vi sadi mein banwaya gaya tha. Yah shahar ka ek pramukh paryatan sthal hai jo apni rajshahi kalakriti aur itihaas ke liye mashhoor hai. Rajwada ke samne ek sundar garden aur raat ko roshniyon se saja hua mahal manohar drishya prastut karta hai.
