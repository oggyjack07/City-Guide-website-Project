Meetha Talab - Shanti aur Saundarya ka Sthal

Meetha Talab Dewas ka ek mashhoor aur shaant jheel sthal hai jo parivarik ghoomne ke liye bahut lokpriya hai. Yah jheel prakritik saundarya se ghiri hui hai aur log yahaan subah ki walk, evening outing, aur boating ka anand lete hain. Talab ke aaspaas park, fountains aur baithne ke suvidhaayein bhi upalabdh hain. Shaam ke samay yahaan ki lighting aur halka music ek shant aur sukhad anubhav deti hai.
