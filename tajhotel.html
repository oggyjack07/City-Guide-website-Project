Taj Mahal Palace - Mumbai's Iconic Luxury Hotel

The Taj Mahal Palace is Mumbai's most iconic luxury hotel, standing majestically opposite the Gateway of India since 1903. Built by industrialist Jamsetji Tata, this architectural marvel blends Indo-Saracenic, Oriental, and Florentine styles. The hotel features 285 rooms including 46 suites, world-class restaurants like Wasabi by Morimoto and Masala Kraft, and the renowned Jiva Spa. The Taj has hosted royalty, presidents, and celebrities for over a century. Its grand central dome, cantilever staircase, and art collection featuring over 5,000 pieces make it a cultural landmark. The hotel gained global attention during the 2008 Mumbai attacks when it became a site of terrorist siege, demonstrating remarkable resilience in its subsequent restoration.
