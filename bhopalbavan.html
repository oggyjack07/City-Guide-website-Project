Bharat Bhavan - Bhopal Ka Sanskritik Kendra

Bharat Bhavan Bhopal ka ek lokpriya kala, sahitya aur sangeet ka kendr hai. Isay 1982 mein sthapit kiya gaya tha aur yah Bharat ke sabse bade cultural complexes mein se ek hai. Yahaan painting gallery, auditorium, library, open-air amphitheatre aur workshop spaces uplabdh hain. Kala premi log yahaan desh aur videsh ke kalakaron ki kala dekhne aate hain.
