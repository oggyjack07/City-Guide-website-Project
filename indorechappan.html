56 Dukan - Indore ka Food Heaven

56 Dukan, jise "Chappan Dukan" bhi kaha jata hai, Indore ka sabse mashhoor food street hai. Yah jagah swadisht street food ke liye poore Bharat mein prasiddh hai. Yahan ki pav bhaji, dosas, tandoori chai, aur chatpati chaat logon ka dil jeet leti hai. Shaam ke samay yah jagah roshniyon se jagmagati hai aur har umr ke log yahan maze lete nazar aate hain.
