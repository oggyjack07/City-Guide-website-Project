Taj-ul-Masajid - Bharat ki Sabse Badi Masjid

Taj-ul-Masajid, Bhopal ki ek iconic aur bhavya masjid hai jo na sirf Madhya Pradesh mein balki poore Bharat mein prasiddh hai. Iska arth hai "Masajid ka Taj". Is masjid ka nirmaan 19vi sadi mein shuru hua tha aur kai dashkon ke baad pura hua. Yah masjid apne bade gumbadon, unche minaro aur shaandar Mughal-style architecture ke liye jaani jaati hai. Ramzan ke dauran yahaan ka mahaul aur bhi adhyatmik ban jaata hai. Yah Bhopal aane wale har visitor ke liye ek must-visit sthal hai.
